Other ways to help

Write a letter

Write a letter or card to someone in custody.

Write an encouraging note that places value and worth upon people (young person or adult – male or female) to provide them with hope, encouragement and support.

Letters can be sent to: info@deadlyconnections.org.au or posted to:

Deadly letters of hope and support
PO BOX 6166
Marrickville South NSW 2204

Speaker

Request our Co-Founder & Ambassador to share their story (link for Keenan Mundine on home page and other articles within media tabs) with your audience at a school or event.

Please contact info@deadlyconnections.org.au

In-Kind Support

This could be business, promotional or marketing support or something else. If the type of support you would like to provide is not listed here, please contact us with your suggestions. As a newly established organisation with limited support we sincerely appreciate any pro bono support that we can access to keep our costs down and redirect our existing funds to children, young people, individuals and families who need it the most.

Prizes

Donate goods, services, prizes, gifts, samples or products for various fundraising activities.

Please contact info@deadlyconnections.org.au

Other Donations

We will also accept material donations (e.g. prams, new toys, new bicycles, nappies, car seats, scholarships, school items) OR donate vouchers to our families (Coles/Woolworths)

Send vouchers to: (or we can arrange pick up within Sydney)

Voucher Donations
PO BOX 6166
Marrickville South NSW 2204
