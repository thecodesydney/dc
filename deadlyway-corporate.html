Corporate Support and Sponsorship

Thank you for your interest in supporting Deadly Connections. Your assistance will help us to continue to provide support to our people, families and communities.

A partnership with Deadly Connections lets customers and employees know that you have an ongoing commitment to supporting First Nations people and Communities, and giving back to those in need.

Deadly Connections is an Aboriginal community led, grass roots not-for-profit organisation that provides support, interventions, services and programs to Aboriginal people, families and communities - particularly those who are impacted by the child protection and/or justice systems.

Our corporate partnerships help us to provide programs throughout Australia that will assist Aboriginal people, successfully shape their own futures, navigate life's challenges, begin their journeys of healing and empower participants to take responsibility for their individual development to thrive and become the best version of themselves.

Corporate sponsorship is not a new idea. For many years, businesses have donated money to charitable organisations that serve their community. In fact, many Fortune 500 corporations maintain dedicated corporate social responsibility departments whose sole mission is to identify charitable agencies with which the company will align. For small business owners, pursuing corporate sponsorship opportunities can be a shrewd decision, providing many benefits. https://smallbusiness.chron.com/advantages-corporations-sponsoring-charitable-organizations-503.html

Your commitment to partner with us will help us to support even more individuals, families and Communities.

YOUR BENEFITS WILL INCLUDE:

  1. You will reach a wider demographic
  2. Increased visibility gained throughout the community
  3. Increased brand recognition throughout the community
  4. Increased credibility within the consumer market
  5. Opportunities for staff engagement
  6. Corporate responsibility to deepen your emerging and well-recognised brand through alignment with Deadly Connections
  7. Business image is often enhanced in the public’s perception
  8. Enhance your leadership role in the Australian business community, demonstrating a strong commitment to corporate social responsibility and philanthropy by supporting us
  9. VIP perks for upcoming events (including fundraising dinners)

Your donation will help us to continue to provide support to those who need it by going directly to support our frontline services.

Workplace Giving

A workplace giving program is set up by an employer and is the most tax effective, simple and powerful way that their employees can donate to a charity directly from their salaries.

A workplace giving program is easy for employers to set up and provides many benefits for both companies and employees while supporting a great cause. Employers can also choose to match their employee’s contributions dollar for dollar.

Donations are made through the payroll department, employees don’t need to do anything once they are set up.

Please use the letter below to inform your employer about your intention to financial support Deadly Connections through workplace giving.

Brave Foundation Workplace Giving Letter

Donations of more than two dollars are deducted from an employee’s salary before it is taxed, resulting in an immediate tax saving.

Further information about Workplace Giving can be found on the ATO’s website at:

https://www.ato.gov.au/Individuals/Working/Working-as-an-employee/Workplace-giving-programs/
